My Story

I started baking because I wanted to feed the people I love with something real. What began as a tiny kitchen table operation has grown into a small-batch bakery rooted in the Pacific Northwest.

Every loaf, every pastry, is made by hand in small batches. No shortcuts — just time, good ingredients, and care.
